Chris Weil – Filmmaker

Director Cinematographer Film & Video Editor

Chris Weil [TISCH] (*March 2, 1988, Bad Nauheim) is an independent German Filmmaker and New Media Artist, who lives and works in Italy.

Awards

Biography

After graduating as a Film & Video Editor in 2011 at the ARD.ZDF Media Academy in association with the Bayerischer Rundfunk and the IHK, Christian Tobias Weil starts video editing for the German public broadcasting stations (ARD and BR).

In 2015 he moves to Italy and founds in collaboration with Sara Ferro the creative directors collective ARTOLDO producing Arthouse movies, Feature documentaries, Experimental films and Video Art.

In 2021 the duo joins the Etherea Art Gallery by Virginia Monteverde featuring international artists from all around Europe.

In 2022 their works get selected for the AdAC ARCHIVIO DARTE CONTEMPORANEA Universit degli Studi di Genova.
